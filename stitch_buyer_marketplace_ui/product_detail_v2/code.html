The Story Behind the Piece

Hand-thrown in our Portland studio, the Artisanal Speckled Vessel represents a perfect marriage of wabi-sabi philosophy and modern structural design. Each piece is crafted using a unique blend of reclaim clay and locally sourced volcanic sand, giving it a texture that is both raw and refined.

The speckles are a result of naturally occurring manganese in the clay, which blooms during the high-fire process. No two vessels are identical, making yours a truly singular addition to your home.

  • Sustainably sourced materials
